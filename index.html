Strategic Focus Areas

Minerals & Critical Materials

Eagle Trident Ventures secures access to critical minerals as a cornerstone of national security, technological leadership, and economic resilience in the 21st century. We invest in mining, processing, recycling, and supply chain ventures focused on lithium, rare earths, uranium, and other strategic materials. Geopolitical risks and surging demand from EVs, renewables, and defense applications have exposed dangerous vulnerabilities in global supply chains.

Significant opportunities exist in both developed and emerging jurisdictions as nations seek to diversify away from concentrated supply chain sources. Our investments strengthen supply security while targeting attractive risk-adjusted returns.

Defense Tech & Dual-Use Innovation

Maintaining technological superiority is essential for deterrence and economic competitiveness. We back companies developing dual-use technologies in autonomy, logistics, sensing, secure communications and related fields. The convergence of commercial innovation and defense needs creates powerful platforms that can rapidly scale while addressing urgent capability gaps for the U.S. and its allies.

The global defense tech market is innovating and expanding rapidly, offering substantial commercial and strategic returns. We look for portfolio companies that will contribute directly to national security while building valuable intellectual property and recurring revenue streams for large-scale general industry adoption.

Energy & Critical Infrastructure

Reliable, resilient energy and infrastructure systems underpin modern economies and national security. Our team has decades of experience leading in the energy and infrastructure space — leading multi-billion dollar investments and development projects. We invest across the spectrum — traditional energy assets, transmission, grid modernization, renewables integration, and hybrid solutions. Energy infrastructure must be pragmatic and reliable; over-reliance on intermittent sources without proper baseload and storage creates systemic risks.

This sector offers durable cash flows, inflation protection, and meaningful contribution to energy security. We target projects that deliver both attractive financial returns and long-term societal resilience.
