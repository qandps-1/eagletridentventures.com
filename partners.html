Our Partners

Leadership driving strategic investments in minerals, defense tech, and energy & infrastructure.

Bob Smith

Founding Partner | Principal & CEO, Adobe Capital Partners and Infrastructure X

A 30-year energy, infrastructure, and capital markets executive, Bob brings deep expertise in private equity, venture capital, deal structuring, and complex transactions. He previously served as EVP, General Counsel, and Chief Development Officer of Pinnacle West Capital (NYSE: PNW), and led Columbia Pipeline Group through a $13 billion sale following its double IPO spin-off from NiSource.

As President of El Dorado Investment Corporation, PNW's venture capital vehicle, Bob oversaw more than $100 million in investment assets across energy technology, defense tech, advanced computing, AI, fintech, and critical minerals. He currently advises several technology funds including Energy Impact Partners, The Westly Group, AZ VC Fund, and Asterozoa Capital Partners.

Bob's network spans C-suite officers at the largest energy companies in the US and Canada and senior bankers at leading global investment banks. He holds a law degree from The Ohio State University and a bachelor's degree from the University of South Alabama.

Beyond business, Bob chairs the board of Global Action, an international compassion and leader training organization, and is founder of the GoSat Initiative, a multi-ministry effort to embed core Christian principles in geopolitically changing nations.

Bruce Roberts

Partner | CEO & Founder, Trident Solutions

A retired U.S. Army Lieutenant Colonel with nearly three decades of operational experience spanning logistics, intelligence, infantry, and public relations, Bruce brings rare cross-domain expertise to defense and infrastructure engagements. His final assignments included service as a Special Operations Battalion Commander and Division Assistant Operations Officer.

After retiring in 2021, Bruce founded August Mission, a nonprofit that supported U.S. citizens and Afghan allies during the U.S. withdrawal from Afghanistan. As CEO of Trident Solutions, he provides strategic leadership and cross-domain coordination, guiding complex engagements with clarity and operational precision across defense, dual-use technology, and critical infrastructure sectors.

Nicholas Fowler

Partner | President & Founder, Trident Solutions

An operational leader with more than 20 years of experience across defense, aviation, and humanitarian operations in high-risk and politically sensitive environments, Nicholas has built and led partnerships across government, NGOs, and private ventures in austere conditions worldwide.

As President of Trident Solutions, Nicholas leads international strategy, coordinates high-value introductions, and manages senior-level engagements with defense and government stakeholders across Europe and beyond. His operational depth and global network make him a trusted bridge between strategic vision and on-the-ground execution.

John Loudon

Partner

A seasoned strategic advisor with deep experience in international business development, defense, and cross-sector investments. John brings a global network and a proven track record of building high-value partnerships across complex, multi-jurisdictional environments.

Antonio Misuraca

Partner

A globally connected humanitarian leader and civilian private contractor, Antonio brings over two decades of experience mobilizing high-net-worth networks, corporate sponsors, and special operations professionals in support of veterans, disaster relief, and community initiatives worldwide.

Antonio has spearheaded major fundraising campaigns for organizations including Make-A-Wish Foundation, Global Empowerment Mission, Hire Our Heroes, and August Mission — consistently leveraging relationships with world leaders, corporate titans, professional athletes, and celebrities to drive impact. Sponsors of his events have included McLaren, Pepsi, Under Armour, Anheuser-Busch, Hublot, and Carnival Cruises.

In his role as a civilian contractor alongside retired U.S. Special Operations operators, Antonio has provided solutions in global conflict zones for over a decade. He holds an FAA student pilot's license and has completed training in Tactical Combat Casualty Care (TCCC), Tactical Driving, Survival, Land Navigation, and Emergency Vehicle Operation. He currently serves in an advisory capacity to the Community Police Relations Foundation, working to strengthen trust between law enforcement and inner-city communities.

Preston Smith

Partner | CIO, Adobe Capital Partners & Principal, Transform Capital Partners

Preston oversees a diversified investment portfolio spanning technology, AI, defense, data centers, healthcare technology, real estate, quantum computing, and advanced energy. With a decade of experience in stock and options trading and a robust real estate portfolio across Arizona, Texas, and North Carolina, he brings a disciplined approach to capital allocation and long-term value creation.

As a Principal of Transform Capital Partners, a joint family office, Preston drives transformative investment opportunities across high-growth sectors, leveraging deep expertise in portfolio construction and emerging technology markets. He holds a bachelor's degree from Arizona State University.

Beyond investing, Preston serves as Director of Operations for the Sustainable Missions Project, empowering community leaders in developing nations through bi-vocational training and mission-driven initiatives. He also serves as director of the G6 World Changers Group, a network of high-net-worth Christian business leaders driving global impact through philanthropy and mission-focused endeavors.
